History

Founded in 2005, Riverside Rapids White Water Rafting began as a small family-owned business with a mission to share the thrill of river adventure. Our founders, passionate outdoor enthusiasts and experienced guides, recognized the incredible potential of our local waterways and set out to create a safe, fun, and eco-friendly white water rafting experience for everyone. Over the years, Riverside Rapids has grown from a modest operation serving a handful of local adventurers to the leading provider of white water rafting services in the region. We have expanded our fleet, trained a team of experienced guides, and invested in the latest safety equipment to ensure every trip is both exciting and safe. Our commitment to sustainability and environmental stewardship has earned us recognition and numerous awards, helping us build strong relationships with local communities and conservation organizations. Today, Riverside Rapids is proud to serve thousands of satisfied customers each year, offering unforgettable adventures that combine adrenaline, nature, and safety.
