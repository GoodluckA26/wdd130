White Water Adventurers

Join us for an unforgettable whitewater rafting experience! Our trips are designed for adventurers of all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned rafters. Whether you're looking for a thrilling ride or a scenic float, we have the perfect trip for you.

It's our name, our focus and our passion

Here at White Water Adventurers our goal is to provide you with a quality rafting experience. We love the outdoors. It is our passion. Let us introduce you to our sport and turn you from a first time rafter to a life time adventurer.


We gladly accept reservations year round for rafting trips March-October. We raft 3 river sections to suit your needs. Choose from:

  • Middle (Class I-II) - Perfect For Families with smaller children

  • Lower (Class III) - Perfect For Teens, and New to Experienced Rafters

  • Upper (Class IV-V) - Perfect For Extreme Adventurers

    • You will forget the stresses of everyday life by spending a day on the river with your friends and family in the heart of the Accra Highlands. Experience a true whitewater getaway with White Water Adventurers, right here in Accra, Ghana.
